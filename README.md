# 👨‍💼 Human Resources Analytics Dashboard

An interactive **HR Analytics Dashboard** developed using **Tableau** to analyze employee demographics, workforce distribution, hiring trends, salary insights, and performance metrics. This dashboard helps HR teams make data-driven decisions through intuitive visualizations and interactive filters.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The Human Resources Analytics Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of an organization's workforce by transforming raw employee data into meaningful business insights. It enables HR professionals to monitor workforce composition, understand employee demographics, evaluate compensation trends, and identify key organizational patterns.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze workforce demographics
- Monitor hiring and termination trends
- Compare department-wise employee distribution
- Understand salary distribution across education levels
- Evaluate employee performance
- Analyze employee distribution across locations
- Support HR decision-making using interactive dashboards

## 💡 Key Insights

- A total of **8,950 employees** have been hired.
- **7,984 employees** are currently active.
- The **Operations** department has the highest number of employees.
- The workforce consists of **54% male** and **46% female** employees.
- Bachelor's degree holders form the largest education group.
- Employees with higher educational qualifications generally earn higher salaries.
- Salary tends to increase with age and senior job roles.
- Employee distribution varies across multiple branch locations.
